About the Dataset

  • Age: Age of the patient (safe range: 30–60)
  • Sex: M (Male), F (Female)
  • ChestPainType: Types: ATA, NAP, ASY, TA
  • RestingBP: Resting blood pressure (90–140 is typical)
  • Cholesterol: Serum cholesterol (100–300 mg/dl is healthy)
  • FastingBS: Fasting blood sugar > 120 mg/dl (1 = true, 0 = false)
  • RestingECG: Normal, ST, or LVH (electrocardiogram results)
  • MaxHR: Maximum heart rate achieved (100–180 bpm)
  • ExerciseAngina: Y = Yes, N = No
  • Oldpeak: ST depression (0–4 normal range)
  • ST_Slope: Up, Flat, or Down (slope of peak exercise ST)
